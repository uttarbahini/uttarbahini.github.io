Uttarbahini Kshetra

Among the religious tourist attractions of Nepal, Uttarbahini Kshetra is a famous, picturesque and legendary area.

Uttarbahini Kshetra is between Malunga Ward No. 1 of Galyang Municipality of Syangja District and Ward No. 1 of Rambha Rural Municipality of Palpa District.
